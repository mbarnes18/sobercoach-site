How it works

Recovery support that
actually shows up

The first 90 days after leaving treatment are statistically the highest-risk period for relapse. Sober Coach puts structured, human-feeling support in your pocket — and keeps your family meaningfully connected without being intrusive.

Always-On Coach
A coach deeply familiar with recovery culture and 12-step programs. Available at 2am when a craving hits and your sponsor's phone is off. No judgment. No voicemail.
Daily Check-In
A structured but flexible daily reflection tool. Complete a step-through wizard, write a freeform journal entry, or just talk to Coach. All three modes build your recovery record over time.
Family Accountability
With your permission, family members receive warm narrative updates on milestones, difficult days, and stressors — delivered by email, so they don't need the app. You control everything.
The hardest part of loving someone in recovery isn't the fear. It's not knowing what to say, when to step in, and when to let them find their footing. Sober Coach helps you show up at the right moments.

Safety first
Four-level risk framework
Normal, Elevated, High, and Emergency — each with defined Coach behavior and escalation posture.
Always escalates to humans
When risk is detected, Coach surfaces your sponsor, family contacts, 988, and SAMHSA. It's designed to hand off, not hold on.
Privacy-first by design
Family updates require explicit opt-in from you. You control what's shared, with whom, and can revoke at any time.
Built for trust

Safety is the
crown jewel

Coach never diagnoses, never advises on medication, never shames a slip, and never pretends a crisis isn't a crisis. Safety logic is the most carefully engineered part of the product.
